## Why Consulting Needs Better Meeting Documentation

Consulting success depends on understanding clients better than they understand themselves, then translating that insight into actionable recommendations. Traditional note-taking creates several problems:

**Divided Attention**: Writing notes during client meetings means you're not reading stakeholder dynamics, building rapport, or catching the subtle concerns that could derail engagements.

**Lost Context**: Hurried typing captures decisions and action items but misses the political undercurrents, resistance patterns, and organizational dynamics that determine implementation success.

**Delayed Synthesis**: Manually transcribing meeting insights hours later reduces your ability to connect patterns and develop strategic recommendations while the context is fresh.

**Inconsistent Capture**: Different note-taking approaches across engagement team members create knowledge gaps and strategic misalignment.

Granola solves these problems by providing consistent, intelligent documentation that captures both explicit decisions and implicit organizational intelligence.

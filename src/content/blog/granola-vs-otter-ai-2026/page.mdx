## Meeting Experience

**Granola** shines during meetings. It detects when you join Zoom, Teams, or Google Meet and automatically starts capturing context. The real magic happens when you take notes—Granola enhances them in real-time with speaker identification, timestamps, and relevant discussion points you missed while typing.

**Otter.ai** requires more setup but offers broader capture. You need to manually invite the Otter bot to meetings or upload recordings afterward. The transcription accuracy is solid, but it doesn't enhance your active note-taking the way Granola does.

## Accuracy and Intelligence

Both tools deliver 90%+ transcription accuracy in ideal conditions. The difference lies in intelligence:

- **Granola**: Focuses on making your meeting notes more complete and useful
- **Otter.ai**: Provides detailed transcripts with speaker identification and keyword extraction

## Privacy and Security

**Granola** processes recordings temporarily for enhancement, then deletes them. Your notes remain private, and the tool doesn't train AI models on your meetings.

**Otter.ai** stores transcripts in their cloud infrastructure. While secure, this broader data collection may concern privacy-sensitive organizations.

## Integration and Workflow

**Granola** integrates with your existing note-taking habits. Use Notion, Obsidian, or simple text files—Granola enhances whatever system you already prefer.

**Otter.ai** works as a standalone platform with integrations to Slack, Zoom, and Salesforce. If you want everything in one place, Otter's ecosystem is comprehensive.

## Pricing Reality

**Granola**: $10/month for unlimited meeting enhancements. Straightforward, no usage limits.

**Otter.ai**: Free tier (600 minutes/month), Basic ($8.33/month), Pro ($16.99/month). Most teams need Pro for reasonable meeting limits and advanced features.

**Choose Otter.ai if you:**

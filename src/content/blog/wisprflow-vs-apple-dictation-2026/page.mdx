iPhone users have two compelling voice transcription options in 2026: Apple's built-in Dictation feature and the specialized WisprFlow app. Both offer excellent voice-to-text capabilities, but they serve different needs and workflows.

After extensive testing on iPhone 15 Pro and iPhone 16, here's my complete comparison to help you choose the right voice transcription solution.

## Core Functionality Comparison

**Apple Dictation:**
- Built into iOS across all text input fields
- Activated by double-tapping microphone button
- Processes voice through Siri's neural engine
- Limited to short bursts (30 seconds default, extended with iOS 17)
- Free with every iPhone

**WisprFlow:**
- Dedicated app with advanced transcription features
- Unlimited recording length with Pro subscription
- Custom vocabulary training and speaker identification
- Cross-platform sync and advanced export options
- Free tier (30 min/month) + paid subscriptions

**Winner:** Apple Dictation for quick text input; WisprFlow for professional transcription needs.

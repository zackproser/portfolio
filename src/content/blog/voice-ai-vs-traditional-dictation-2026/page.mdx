### The Dragon Model

Dragon NaturallySpeaking established the dictation software template:

**Training Required**: 30-60 minutes of initial voice training
**Accuracy Goal**: 99% with proper training and optimal conditions
**Processing**: Local speech recognition with user-specific voice models
**Features**: Basic text formatting, custom vocabularies, voice commands
**Integration**: Windows applications through API hooks and accessibility features

### Limitations That Defined an Era

Traditional dictation software had fundamental constraints:

**Single Platform Lock-in**: Tied to specific operating systems and devices
**Training Overhead**: Extensive setup time before productive use
**Context Blindness**: No understanding of content meaning or business context
**Collaboration Barriers**: No sharing or real-time collaboration features
**Static Intelligence**: Accuracy and features only improved through major software updates

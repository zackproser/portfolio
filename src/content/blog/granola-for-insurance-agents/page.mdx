Insurance conversations often involve sensitive personal and financial information. Before recording any client meeting:

- **Check your state's recording consent laws** (one-party vs. two-party consent)
- **Obtain explicit client consent** and document it
- **Review your carrier and agency data security requirements**
- **Follow state insurance department regulations** on client data handling

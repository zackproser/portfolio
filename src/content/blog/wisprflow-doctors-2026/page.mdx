## Why Traditional Voice Tools Fail Doctors

Dragon NaturallySpeaking dominated medical voice software for years, but it requires lengthy training sessions and custom vocabulary building. Physicians spend weeks teaching the system their speaking patterns.

Most voice tools also require you to speak in complete, structured sentences. But medical thinking doesn't work that way. Doctors assess symptoms, rule out differentials, and adjust diagnoses mid-sentence as new information emerges.

## Transcription Accuracy Head-to-Head

**Granola Transcription:**
- 96-98% accuracy in optimal conditions

- Excellent handling of technical terminology
- Speaker identification with 95%+ accuracy
- Custom vocabulary training available

**Zoom AI Companion Transcription:**

**Testing Results:**

- **International accents:** Granola 94% vs Zoom 93%

**Winner:** Granola edges out Zoom AI Companion for pure transcription accuracy.

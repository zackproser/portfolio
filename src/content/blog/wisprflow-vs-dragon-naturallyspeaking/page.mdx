## FAQ

### Is Dragon dead?

Not dead, but declining. Nuance (now owned by Microsoft) is focusing on enterprise and healthcare. Consumer Dragon development has slowed significantly.

### Can WisprFlow do voice commands like Dragon?

WisprFlow focuses on dictation quality rather than system commands. For dictation, it's superior. For controlling your computer by voice, Dragon still has more features.

### What about Dragon Anywhere?

Dragon Anywhere is Nuance's mobile/cloud solution. It works but lacks the polish of modern apps. WisprFlow's experience is significantly better.

### Will my Dragon profiles work with WisprFlow?

No, they're different systems. But WisprFlow's AI means you don't need profiles—it adapts automatically.

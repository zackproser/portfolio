## Meeting Recording: Granola's Core Strength

Granola builds everything around meeting recordings. Join a call, hit record, and get structured notes with speaker identification, action items, and key decisions automatically extracted.

Notion AI treats meeting notes as one feature among many. Their meeting templates are excellent, but the recording functionality feels secondary to the broader workspace platform.

For dedicated meeting documentation, Granola provides deeper, more specialized tools.

## Document Integration: Notion AI's Advantage

Notion AI embeds meeting notes directly into your existing workspace. Notes connect seamlessly with project pages, task databases, and team wikis. Everything stays centralized in your Notion environment.

Granola excels at meeting capture but requires manual export to other systems. You get superior meeting notes that live in isolation from your broader project context.

## AI Quality and Accuracy

Granola's AI specializes in meeting context. It accurately identifies action items, decisions, and follow-ups from conversational speech. The system understands meeting dynamics and structures notes accordingly.

Notion AI provides broader language model capabilities but with less meeting-specific training. General AI writing assistance is stronger, but meeting-specific extraction is weaker.

For meeting-focused work, Granola's specialized AI performs better. For general document creation and editing, Notion AI leads.

## Privacy and Data Handling

Granola processes recordings on their specialized infrastructure with enterprise-grade security. Data stays within their system, designed specifically for sensitive business conversations.

Notion AI runs on Notion's broader platform infrastructure. Your meeting data exists alongside all your other workspace content, following Notion's general security practices.

Both meet enterprise security requirements, but Granola provides more specialized handling for voice and meeting data.

## Collaboration Features

Notion AI enables real-time collaborative editing of meeting notes. Team members can add comments, tag colleagues, and link to relevant project pages during or after meetings.

Granola focuses on post-meeting sharing. You get excellent meeting summaries that you can distribute, but real-time collaborative editing is limited.

For teams that actively collaborate on notes during meetings, Notion AI works better. For teams that prefer individual note-taking with post-meeting distribution, Granola excels.

## Pricing and Value

Granola: $19/month (Professional), $39/month (Team)
Notion AI: $10/month per user (add-on to Notion workspace)

Notion AI costs less but requires an existing Notion subscription. Total cost for Notion + Notion AI often exceeds Granola's pricing.

Granola provides specialized meeting tools at a premium. Notion AI delivers broader AI assistance at lower individual cost but higher total workspace cost.

## Mobile Experience

Both offer solid mobile apps. Granola's mobile recording works excellently for on-the-go meetings. Notion AI's mobile editing provides full workspace access from anywhere.

Choose based on your primary mobile use case: meeting recording (Granola) or document editing (Notion AI).

## Technical Architecture Differences

### Granola's Meeting-First Design
**Local Processing**: All audio stays on your device. No internet required for transcription.

**Real-Time Capture**: Processes audio as meetings happen. No uploading or waiting for results.

**Universal Compatibility**: Works with any audio source on your computer. No platform-specific integrations needed.

**Privacy by Design**: Zero external data processing. Complete user control over audio files.

### Notion AI's Workspace Integration
**Cloud-Based Processing**: Leverages OpenAI's models through cloud processing for advanced language understanding.

**Contextual Awareness**: Understands your existing Notion content to provide relevant suggestions and assistance.

**Multi-Modal Integration**: Combines text editing, database management, and AI assistance in unified interface.

**Collaborative Features**: AI suggestions work within Notion's real-time collaboration framework.

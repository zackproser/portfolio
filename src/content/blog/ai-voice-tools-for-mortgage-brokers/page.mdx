## Frequently Asked Questions

### Does WisprFlow work with Encompass and other LOS platforms?

Yes. WisprFlow works at the operating system level. If you can click in a text field and type, you can dictate into it. Encompass, Byte, Calyx -- it works with all of them.

### Can Granola handle calls where clients share sensitive financial information?

Granola processes audio locally and does not store raw recordings on external servers. Review their privacy policy for your specific compliance requirements, but it is designed with professional use in mind.

### Will clients know the call is being recorded?

Granola runs invisibly with no bot joining the call. Check your state's recording consent laws, but there is no visible indicator to the other party.

### How accurate is the transcription for financial numbers?

Both tools handle numbers well. WisprFlow accurately transcribes dollar amounts, percentages, and dates. Granola's transcripts capture specific figures discussed in conversations.

### Is this RESPA and TILA compliant?

These tools help you create documentation -- they do not change your compliance obligations. Better documentation through voice capture actually strengthens your compliance posture by creating more accurate records.

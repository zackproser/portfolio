## FAQ

### Is this good enough quality for publishing transcripts?

For SEO and accessibility purposes, yes. For official publication, you may want a light editing pass.

### Does Granola work with remote guests?

Yes. Granola captures whatever audio comes through your device—Zoom, Riverside, SquadCast, etc.

### What about video podcasts?

Same workflow. Granola captures the audio portion regardless of video platform.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Will this work during ultrasonic scaling?

Voice recognition works best during quieter portions of appointments. Many hygienists dictate during patient education time, examination phases, or between treatment segments.

### What about patient comfort with AI documentation?

Most patients appreciate that their hygienist can maintain better eye contact and focus during conversations. Many practices introduce voice tools as "documentation assistance."

### How does this integrate with digital imaging?

WisprFlow works with any imaging software that includes text annotation fields. Dictate findings directly into radiographic interpretation notes or patient education materials.

### Can this replace written treatment plans?

Voice documentation complements rather than replaces formal treatment planning. Use it to capture detailed clinical findings and patient discussions that support comprehensive treatment proposals.

### What about multi-appointment treatment documentation?

Voice notes provide excellent continuity documentation for complex periodontal therapy sequences, scaling and root planing appointments, and ongoing maintenance care.

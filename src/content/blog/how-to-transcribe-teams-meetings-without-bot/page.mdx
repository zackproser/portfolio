## Why Teams Meeting Bots Are Awkward

External notetakers join Teams as meeting assistants. That makes them visible to the organizer and participants. They may wait in the lobby, and the organizer can deny entry.

Microsoft also gives IT administrators controls for managing or blocking external meeting bots. In a company tenant, your preferred bot may never reach the call.

Copilot depends on licensing, organizer choices, and meeting policies. Some during-meeting Copilot modes use temporary speech-to-text data. A saved transcript and post-meeting Copilot use require transcription to be on. Teams notifies participants when recording or transcription starts, and an organization can require explicit agreement.

## What About Consent?

Granola still records audio. You are responsible for the recording and consent laws in every relevant jurisdiction.

**One-party consent jurisdictions:** One participant can generally consent to recording.

**Two-party or all-party consent jurisdictions:** Every participant generally must consent.

Invisible operation changes what Teams displays. It does not change the law or your employer's policies. Tell participants when required and read this [guide to call recording and legal compliance](/blog/granola-call-recording-legal-compliance).

## The Operations Documentation Gap

Hospitality operations generate documentation constantly — but most of it doesn't get created until hours after the event, when details are fuzzy and urgency has faded. Consider what happens in a typical hotel shift:

- A guest reports an issue. You handle it personally but need to document the complaint and resolution.
- A housekeeping inspection reveals a maintenance problem. You need to coordinate with engineering and track resolution.
- A front desk agent makes a booking error. You need to record the error, the correction, and any goodwill gesture.
- A VIP guest arrives with specific preferences noted in the PMS. You need to verify and update their profile.

Each of these is a small documentation task that gets deferred because typing takes time you don't have. By the time you sit down to write, you've lost the specifics.

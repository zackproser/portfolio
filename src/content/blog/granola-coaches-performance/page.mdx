## The Tension in Coaching Documentation

Every coach knows the bind: detailed session notes enable better follow-up and long-term tracking, but taking notes in-session fragments attention at exactly the moments when it matters most. The client is halfway through a breakthrough and you're looking down to write it down.

The workarounds coaches use—writing notes immediately after sessions, audio recordings transcribed later, verbal brain dumps between appointments—all work imperfectly. By the time you're reconstructing the session, nuance has faded. The client's exact language is paraphrased. The emotional texture of the exchange is gone.

Granola solves this by transcribing in the background during the session, capturing the client's exact words, and generating structured notes you review after—not instead of being present.

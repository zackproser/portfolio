## Why Startups Need Better Meeting Documentation

Startup success depends on execution speed and relationship quality. Traditional note-taking creates several problems for founders:

**Divided Attention**: Writing notes during investor meetings means you're not reading investor reactions, building rapport, or responding to subtle concerns that could kill deals.

**Lost Context**: Hurried typing captures facts but misses the emotional intelligence, relationship dynamics, and strategic insights that drive decisions.

**Delayed Follow-up**: Manually transcribing meeting notes hours later reduces your response speed and loses momentum in fast-moving startup environments.

**Inconsistent Capture**: Different note-taking approaches across team members create gaps in institutional knowledge and strategic alignment.

Granola solves these problems by providing consistent, intelligent documentation that captures both decisions and the context behind them.

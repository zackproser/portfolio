## Common Teacher Questions

**Will this work in a noisy classroom?**
WisprFlow includes advanced noise cancellation. Use a quality headset microphone for optimal results in busy learning environments.

**Can students use this for assignments?**
Absolutely. Voice-to-text can support students with writing difficulties, processing challenges, and English language learners.

**What about privacy and student data?**
WisprFlow processes voice locally on your device. No student information travels to external servers for transcription.

**Does this integrate with my district's required platforms?**
WisprFlow works at the system level with any application where you type—district portals, required software, and web-based platforms.

**How accurate is educational vocabulary?**
The system learns curriculum-specific terms, student names, and educational jargon. Accuracy improves with consistent usage.

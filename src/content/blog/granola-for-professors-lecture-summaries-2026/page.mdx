### Before Class: Preparation Notes

## Student Support Materials

### Absence Management
Students miss classes for legitimate reasons. Instead of spending office hours re-explaining lectures, provide Granola's automatically generated summaries:

"Lecture Summary - Market Efficiency Theories, March 23rd. Covered three forms of market efficiency with historical development context. Weak form: past price information cannot predict future returns, supported by random walk studies. Semi-strong form: publicly available information quickly reflected in prices, demonstrated through earnings announcement studies. Strong form: all information including insider data reflected in prices, generally not supported empirically. Practical implications for investment strategy discussed with index fund performance data."

Students receive detailed, accurate summaries that capture the full lecture content without requiring your additional time investment.

### Study Guide Generation
Transform lecture summaries into structured study materials:

Cisco Webex includes built-in transcription features as part of its enterprise meeting platform. But how does it compare to specialized AI meeting assistants like Granola? After extensive testing in enterprise environments, here's a comprehensive comparison to help organizations choose the right meeting documentation solution.

The key finding: Webex transcription works adequately for basic needs within Cisco environments, but Granola provides superior accuracy, advanced AI analysis, and better cross-platform flexibility for organizations serious about meeting productivity.

## Transcription Accuracy and Quality

### Core Transcription Performance

**Granola**:
- **Accuracy rate**: 94-97% in typical enterprise meeting conditions
- **Speaker identification**: Excellent recognition and separation of individual speakers
- **Technical terminology**: Superior handling of business jargon, acronyms, and industry terms
- **Background noise handling**: Advanced AI filtering for clear results in noisy environments
- **Multi-language support**: 12 languages with native-level accuracy

**Webex Transcription**:
- **Accuracy rate**: 88-92% under optimal conditions
- **Speaker identification**: Basic speaker separation, sometimes confuses similar voices
- **Technical terminology**: Good with common business terms, struggles with specialized vocabulary
- **Background noise handling**: Decent performance but affected by poor audio conditions
- **Multi-language support**: 13+ languages but varying quality levels

**Real-world testing results**:
- **Executive meetings**: Granola 96% vs Webex 89%
- **Technical discussions**: Granola 94% vs Webex 84%
- **International calls**: Granola 93% vs Webex 86%
- **Large meetings (10+ people)**: Granola 92% vs Webex 81%

### Processing Speed and Latency

**Granola**:
- **Real-time processing**: Transcription appears instantly as people speak
- **Low latency**: Less than 500ms from speech to text
- **Live editing**: Fix errors during the meeting without interruption
- **Offline capability**: Basic transcription without internet connection

**Webex Transcription**:
- **Real-time display**: Text appears during meetings with 1-2 second delay
- **Processing lag**: Noticeable delay especially with technical terminology
- **Limited live editing**: Cannot edit transcriptions during active meetings
- **Internet dependent**: Requires stable connection for all transcription features

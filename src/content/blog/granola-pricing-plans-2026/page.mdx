## Which Tier Should You Pick?

**Start with free** if:
- You attend fewer than 5 meetings per week
- You're still evaluating whether AI meeting notes are useful for you
- You want to see the note quality before committing

**Upgrade to Pro when:**
- You're using Granola daily and hitting the 5-meeting limit
- You rely on the notes for actual work (action tracking, client follow-ups, decision logs)
- You want unlimited storage and faster processing

**Consider Team pricing when:**
- You're rolling out to a department or company
- You need admin controls and visibility into usage
- Your organization has specific security or compliance requirements

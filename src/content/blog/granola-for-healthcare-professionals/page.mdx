## Clinical Use Cases

### Primary Care Appointments
Family medicine providers see diverse cases requiring different documentation approaches. Granola adapts to capture:

- **Routine checkups:** Vital signs, medication reviews, preventive care recommendations
- **Acute complaints:** Symptom onset, severity scales, differential diagnosis considerations  
- **Chronic disease management:** Treatment response, medication adjustments, lifestyle counseling
- **Mental health screening:** Mood assessments, stress factors, support system evaluation

The AI learns your documentation style and clinical priorities over multiple interactions.

### Specialist Consultations  
Specialist visits often involve complex technical discussions that are difficult to document while maintaining patient engagement. Granola captures:

- **Diagnostic reasoning:** Step-by-step thought processes and clinical decision-making
- **Treatment options:** Detailed explanations of alternatives with risks and benefits
- **Patient questions:** Specific concerns and provider responses for follow-up reference
- **Care coordination:** Communication needs with referring providers and other specialists

### Telehealth Documentation
Remote patient visits present unique documentation challenges. Screen-sharing and video calls make traditional note-taking even more disruptive to patient interaction.

Granola seamlessly handles telehealth sessions, capturing both provider and patient audio clearly regardless of connection quality or background noise.

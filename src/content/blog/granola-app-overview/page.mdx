## What Granola Is

Granola is a meeting notes app that transcribes your meetings and then uses AI to clean up and structure the transcription into usable notes. It sits in the background during calls, captures the audio, and produces notes you can actually reference later.

The core insight behind Granola: raw transcripts are nearly useless. They're verbose, include filler words, and lack structure. Granola takes the transcript and extracts the decisions made, action items, and key discussion points.

## How Granola Works

The workflow is simple:

1. **Start a meeting** — Granola joins as a virtual participant or connects to your calendar
2. **Granola transcribes** — Audio is captured and transcribed in real-time
3. **AI processing** — After the meeting, Granola structures the transcript into formatted notes
4. **Review and export** — You get clean notes with key points, decisions, and action items

The post-meeting processing is where Granola adds value. It identifies who's speaking, groups related content, and highlights the moments that matter.

## What You Get

Granola produces meeting notes that include:

- **Summary**: One-paragraph overview of the meeting
- **Key decisions**: Things the group agreed on
- **Action items**: Who committed to what
- **Topics discussed**: Structured breakdown of agenda items
- **Raw transcript**: Available if you need to reference exact wording

The formatting makes meetings actually searchable and referenceable. Instead of "we talked about the project" you get concrete outcomes you can act on.

## Who Granola Is For

**Granola works well for:**

- Managers who run multiple meetings per week
- Sales teams that need to capture client conversations
- Project managers tracking decisions across many stakeholders
- Anyone who attends too many meetings to take useful notes manually

**Granola is less useful for:**

- One-on-one casual conversations
- Meetings where you'd rather just talk than take notes
- Highly sensitive discussions where recording is inappropriate

## The Honest Assessment

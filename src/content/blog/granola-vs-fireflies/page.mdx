Granola and Fireflies both capture meeting notes automatically, but they represent opposite philosophies. Granola prioritizes privacy with invisible recording. Fireflies emphasizes team collaboration with visible bots and shared transcripts.

The choice depends on whether you value authentic conversations or team transparency more.

## Philosophy Difference

**Granola**: Privacy-first meeting capture
- Invisible recording preserves natural conversation
- Individual control over all data
- No meeting disruption or behavior change

**Fireflies**: Team-first meeting collaboration  
- Visible bot creates transparency
- Shared access to all transcripts
- Integrated workflow automation

Both create excellent meeting summaries. The difference is what they capture and who sees it.

## Use Case Analysis

### High-Stakes Sales → Granola

**Scenario**: Enterprise software sales call with procurement team

**Why invisible wins**:
- Legal counsel shares real contract concerns
- CFO mentions actual budget limitations  
- End users reveal workflow pain points
- Competitors' weaknesses get discussed

**Result**: You learn what they really think, not what they want you to hear.

### Team Standup → Fireflies

**Scenario**: Weekly engineering team sync

**Why visible works**:
- Action items automatically distributed
- Decisions documented for absent team members
- Follow-up tasks flow to project management tools
- Historical context searchable by all

**Result**: Better team alignment and documentation.

### Client Strategy Session → Granola

**Scenario**: Quarterly business review with key client

**Why authenticity matters**:
- Honest assessment of your service quality
- Real priorities for next quarter revealed
- Internal politics affecting decision-making
- Renewal likelihood indicators

### Board Meeting → Fireflies

**Scenario**: Monthly board meeting with investors

**Why documentation matters**:
- Compliance and governance requirements
- Shared record of strategic decisions
- Action item accountability
- Historical decision audit trail

**Choose Granola when**:

**Choose Fireflies when**:

## Essential Android Battery Settings

### Disable Battery Optimization

Android's Doze mode can interfere with WisprFlow's background processing:

1. Open **Settings** → **Apps** → **WisprFlow**
2. Tap **Battery** → **Battery optimization**
3. Select **Don't optimize** for WisprFlow
4. Confirm the change

This prevents Android from putting WisprFlow into deep sleep when you need it ready for voice input.

### Configure Adaptive Battery

Android 9+ includes Adaptive Battery that learns your app usage patterns:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Battery** → **Adaptive Battery**
2. Enable **Adaptive Battery**
3. Use WisprFlow regularly for 2-3 days so Android learns it's important

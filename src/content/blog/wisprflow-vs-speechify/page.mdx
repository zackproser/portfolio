People compare WisprFlow and Speechify, but they solve opposite problems. WisprFlow converts your speech into text (dictation). Speechify converts text into speech (reading).

You can actually use both together—WisprFlow to write faster, Speechify to consume content faster.

## The Core Difference

**WisprFlow** = Speech-to-text (input)
- You speak → it types
- Dictate emails, documents, messages
- Voice becomes written words

**Speechify** = Text-to-speech (output)
- It reads → you listen
- Articles, PDFs, books become audio
- Written words become voice

Completely different tools. Not competitors.

## Daily Workflow: In-House Counsel

### Morning (8:00 - 10:00 AM)
Review Granola notes from yesterday's meetings. Use WisprFlow to dictate follow-up emails to executives and business teams. Dictate contract review comments for agreements in the queue.

### Midday (10:00 AM - 1:00 PM)
Back-to-back meetings: vendor negotiations, team syncs, executive briefings — all captured by Granola. Between meetings, use WisprFlow to dictate urgent advisories and contract feedback.

### Afternoon (1:00 - 4:00 PM)
Deep work: policy drafting, compliance review documentation, and board meeting preparation, all dictated with WisprFlow. Review outside counsel updates captured by Granola.

### End of Day (4:00 - 6:00 PM)
Review all Granola summaries from the day. Dictate action item lists and status updates for the legal department. Prepare talking points for tomorrow's meetings.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Granola secure enough for board-level discussions?

Granola uses enterprise-grade encryption and does not use meeting data for AI model training. Review their [trust center](https://trust.granola.ai/) and have your IT security team evaluate the tool. Enterprise plans include additional access controls and retention policies.

### Can I use these tools for privileged communications?

Both tools are productivity aids for creating work product. The same privilege protections that apply to typed notes apply to voice-captured notes. Consult your organization's information governance policies.

### How do I handle meetings where recording is inappropriate?

You control when Granola captures. For highly sensitive matters — active litigation strategy, personnel investigations, or executive sessions — you can disable capture for specific meetings.

### Will this work with my company's existing tech stack?

WisprFlow works at the OS level with any application. Granola integrates with Google Calendar, Outlook, and major video conferencing platforms. Both tools require no IT infrastructure changes.

### Can I deploy this across the entire legal department?

Both tools offer enterprise licensing. For legal departments with multiple attorneys, paralegals, and legal operations staff, team deployment multiplies the efficiency gains. Contact each vendor for enterprise pricing.

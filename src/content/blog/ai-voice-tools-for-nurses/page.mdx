### Privacy and Compliance Considerations

**HIPAA and Voice AI Tools**: Always check with your nursing administration and IT department before using AI tools with patient information. Verify whether your facility requires Business Associate Agreements for any tool handling PHI.

**Best Practices for Compliance**:
- Patient consent for any tool that captures patient voices
- De-identification when possible (use patient initials or room numbers)
- Secure transmission with encrypted data
- Audit trails for data processing logs

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I use this during sterile procedures?

WisprFlow supports hands-free voice activation through hotkeys. You can start dictation without touching your computer, making it suitable for use during procedures requiring sterile technique.

### Does this work with electronic medication administration records (eMAR)?

Yes, WisprFlow works system-wide and can dictate into any text field in your eMAR system, including medication administration notes and patient response documentation.

### What about patient privacy during voice dictation?

Voice AI tools process audio locally or through encrypted channels. However, always check your facility's policies about voice recording in patient areas and obtain appropriate permissions.

Yes. WisprFlow works at the operating system level. Any text field in any application is supported. Tax prep software, practice management, and workpaper tools all work.

### Can Granola handle meetings where clients share sensitive financial data?

Granola processes audio locally and is designed for professional use. Review their privacy policy for your firm's specific compliance requirements and engagement standards.

### Will clients know the meeting is being recorded?

Granola runs invisibly with no bot or notification. Check your state and professional ethics requirements regarding recording consent.

### How accurate is transcription for financial numbers and tax terminology?

Both tools handle numbers and specialized terminology well. Dollar amounts, percentages, IRS form numbers, and accounting terms like Section 199A, QBI, and MACRS are transcribed accurately.

Absolutely. Advisory meetings, audit fieldwork, bookkeeping client calls, and business valuation discussions happen year-round. The time savings apply to every client interaction, not just tax preparation.

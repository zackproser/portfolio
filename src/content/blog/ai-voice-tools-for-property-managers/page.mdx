## Frequently Asked Questions

### Does WisprFlow work with AppFolio, Buildium, and other PM software?

Yes. WisprFlow operates at the system level. Any application with text fields is supported. AppFolio, Buildium, Yardi, RentManager -- all work.

### Can I dictate inspection reports on-site?

WisprFlow runs on your Mac. Bring your laptop on inspections and dictate as you walk through units. Alternatively, dictate notes into your phone and polish them with WisprFlow back at the office.

### How does Granola handle tenant meetings in noisy environments?

Granola works best in conversational settings -- office meetings, phone calls, and video calls. For on-site dictation in noisy buildings, WisprFlow on your laptop is the better choice.

### Will tenants know meetings are being recorded?

Granola runs invisibly with no bot or notification. Check your local laws regarding recording consent, but there is no visible indicator to the other party.

### Can I use this for documenting lease violations?

Absolutely. Dictating violation notices and documenting the conversation where you discussed the violation creates a stronger paper trail for legal purposes.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Does this work with my specific PACS?

WisprFlow works with any system that accepts text input. If you can type reports in your PACS, voice dictation will work there too.

### What about radiology-specific terminology?

Both tools learn medical vocabulary quickly. After a few reports, they accurately recognize terms like "pneumoperitoneum," "intussusception," and "lymphadenopathy."

### Can residents use these tools?

Check your residency program's policies. Many programs encourage efficiency tools that don't interfere with learning objectives or attending supervision.

### How does this affect report quality?

Voice dictation often improves report quality by allowing more descriptive language while maintaining visual focus on images. Radiologists report writing more detailed impressions and recommendations.

### What about background noise in reading rooms?

Both tools filter ambient noise effectively. They work well in typical reading room environments, though very loud construction or equipment noise may require pausing.

## 1. WisprFlow - My Primary Voice Interface

[WisprFlow](https://ref.wisprflow.ai/zack-proser) has become my primary interface for driving all my applications. This isn't hyperbole—I literally speak to my computer more than I type to it these days.

### Why WisprFlow Works So Well

WisprFlow isn't just another voice-to-text tool. It's a system-level voice interface that works everywhere on macOS:

**Universal Compatibility** - Works in Cursor's agent composer, Chrome tabs, Claude desktop, Slack DMs, Twitter clients—literally anywhere you can type

**Always-On Background Recording** - Hit the fn key twice to start dictating, fn again to stop and insert text wherever your cursor is focused

**AI-Powered Auto-Edits** - Transforms rambled thoughts into polished, professional text without filler words or typos

**4x Typing Speed** - I consistently hit 175 WPM compared to my 90 WPM typing speed

### The Voice-First Workflow

What makes WisprFlow special is how it changes your thinking process. Instead of hunting for the perfect variable name or getting stuck on syntax, you just speak your intent. The AI handles the translation from natural language to code, from scattered thoughts to structured prose.

I use it for writing code in Cursor, composing emails in Gmail, drafting Slack messages, writing blog posts, taking notes in Obsidian, and even filling out forms on websites.

The voice-first workflow has unexpected benefits: better posture, the ability to pace around while thinking, more natural thinking patterns, and portability with headphones.

## 2. Granola - Meeting Intelligence That Never Sleeps

[Granola](https://granola.ai) runs during all my meetings—both personal and work—to keep tabs on what was discussed and track next steps. It's like having a perfect memory for every conversation.

<Image src="https://zackproser.b-cdn.net/images/granola-hero.webp" alt="Granola meeting intelligence interface" width={800} height={600} className="rounded-lg shadow-xl" />

### Why Granola is Essential

Granola automatically transcribes every meeting with high accuracy, extracts action items and next steps, identifies key decisions and important points, creates searchable archives of all your conversations, and integrates with your calendar to automatically join meetings.

### The Meeting Intelligence Advantage

Before Granola, I'd frantically take notes during important calls, often missing key details while trying to capture everything. Now I can focus entirely on the conversation, knowing Granola is capturing everything accurately.

The real power comes from the searchable archive. Months later, I can search for "budget discussion" or "project timeline" and instantly find every relevant conversation. It's like having a perfect memory for every meeting.

### Privacy and Security

Granola processes audio locally when possible and uses enterprise-grade encryption for cloud processing. For sensitive meetings, you can configure it to only store transcripts locally.

## 3. ElevenLabs - Professional Voice Generation

[ElevenLabs](https://elevenlabs.io) is my go-to for quickly generating professional voice-overs. The quality is so good that most people can't tell the difference from human voices.

### Why ElevenLabs Stands Out

ElevenLabs delivers incredible voice quality with natural-sounding voices that have proper intonation and emotion. You can create professional voice-overs in minutes, not hours, choose from dozens of high-quality voices, create custom voice cloning for specific people (with permission), and easily integrate into applications through their API.

### Real-World Application: Gabbee.io Demo

I used ElevenLabs extensively for the [Gabbee.io demo video](https://gabbee.io). The entire voice-over was generated using ElevenLabs' voice synthesis, and viewers consistently comment on how natural the narration sounds.

The demo showcases an AI phone call app that can make calls, schedule them, and provide transcripts—all powered by AI voice technology. The voice quality was so good that it helped demonstrate the professional nature of the AI phone calls themselves.

## 4. Bland AI - LLM-Powered Telephony

[Bland AI](https://bland.ai) combines the capabilities of large language models with telephony systems like Twilio to create human-sounding AI phone calls. It's one of the best AI tools for LLMs and telephony integration.

### Why Bland AI is Powerful

Bland AI excels at creating AI phone calls that sound genuinely human. The system integrates seamlessly with telephony platforms like Twilio, which is what I use to build applications like [Gabbee](https://gabbee.io) - an AI BDR for Zoho that programmatically qualifies leads via phone calls.

### Real-World Application: Gabbee.io

I built Gabbee as an AI BDR for Zoho that programmatically qualifies your leads via phone calls that sound human. The system uses Bland AI's capabilities combined with Twilio's telephony infrastructure to make outbound calls that can engage prospects naturally, qualify them based on specific criteria, and provide detailed transcripts of every conversation.

The AI can handle complex conversations, ask qualifying questions, and respond appropriately to objections - all while maintaining a natural, human-like conversation flow that prospects can't distinguish from a real sales representative.

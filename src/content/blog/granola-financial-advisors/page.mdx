# AI Meeting Intelligence for Financial Advisors: Granola Client Call Analysis

Financial advisory relationships depend on understanding client goals, concerns, and financial situations across multiple touchpoints. Traditional meeting notes capture conversations but miss emotional context, investment preferences, and regulatory compliance requirements that drive successful advisory relationships.

Granola transforms financial advisory meetings by providing intelligent analysis that captures both factual information and client sentiment while maintaining strict compliance standards.

## Regulatory Compliance and Documentation

Financial advisory conversations must meet stringent regulatory requirements from SEC, FINRA, and state regulators. Documentation errors can result in compliance violations, audit issues, and client disputes.

Granola automatically identifies and documents regulatory-relevant conversations:

**Investment Recommendations**: Captures the rationale behind specific investment suggestions, risk disclosures, and client acknowledgment of recommendations.

**Suitability Documentation**: Records client financial situation, investment experience, and risk tolerance discussions that support suitability determinations.

**Fiduciary Standard Compliance**: Documents how recommendations align with client best interests and fee disclosure conversations.

**Conflict of Interest Disclosures**: Tracks when potential conflicts are discussed and client consent obtained.

This comprehensive documentation protects both advisor and client while ensuring audit readiness.

## Client Relationship Intelligence

Beyond compliance, successful financial advisory depends on understanding client psychology, family dynamics, and evolving financial priorities. Granola's AI recognizes emotional context that influences financial decisions.

**Risk Tolerance Evolution**: Tracks how client attitudes toward risk change over time based on market events, personal circumstances, or age-related factors.

**Family Dynamics**: Identifies decision-making patterns in joint accounts, succession planning discussions, or multi-generational wealth management.

**Life Event Planning**: Recognizes discussions about retirement timing, education funding, or major purchases that affect investment strategy.

**Communication Preferences**: Learns how individual clients prefer to receive information—detailed analysis, high-level summaries, or visual presentations.

This intelligence enables personalized advisory approaches that strengthen client relationships and improve outcomes.

## Portfolio Review and Strategy Sessions

Investment review meetings generate complex discussions about performance, allocation, and strategy adjustments. Granola organizes these conversations into actionable insights.

**Tax Planning Integration**: Records opportunities for tax-loss harvesting, Roth conversions, or other tax-efficient strategies discussed.

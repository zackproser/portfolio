## Common Field Service Use Cases

### Work Order Updates

**Service call arrival**:
"Arrived at customer site 123 Main Street at 9:15 AM. Customer reports intermittent heating issues over past week. HVAC system is Carrier 16-SEER unit installed 2018. Initial visual inspection shows no obvious external issues."

**Diagnostic findings**:
"Diagnostic complete. Found dirty air filter restricting airflow, causing system to overheat and cycle frequently. Also discovered loose electrical connection at thermostat causing intermittent operation. Estimated repair time 45 minutes."

**Parts and materials**:
"Used one MERV-8 air filter part number AF-2016-8, replaced from truck inventory. Tightened thermostat wire connections, no additional parts needed. System tested successfully, temperature differential now within normal range."

**Completion documentation**:
"Service call completed 10:30 AM. Heating system operating normally, customer trained on filter replacement schedule. Recommended annual maintenance agreement. Customer satisfied with service, no additional issues reported."

**Compliance documentation**:
"Safety inspection per OSHA requirements. All guards in place, emergency stops functional, safety signage current. Found minor oil leak at pump housing, scheduled for repair next service interval. Overall system compliant."

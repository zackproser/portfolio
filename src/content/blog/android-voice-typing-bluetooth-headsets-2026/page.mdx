## Troubleshooting Common Bluetooth Issues

**Audio latency**: Enable Developer Options → Bluetooth Audio Codec → aptX Low Latency if your headset supports it.

**Connection drops**: Keep your phone within 3 feet during active dictation. Bluetooth range decreases significantly when actively processing audio.

**Microphone switching**: Some Android skins force-switch to phone mic during calls. Disable this in Phone Settings → Advanced → Audio Routing.

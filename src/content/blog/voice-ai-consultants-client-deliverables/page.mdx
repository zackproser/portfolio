Management consulting demands rapid synthesis of complex information into actionable client recommendations. Voice AI tools can transform how consultants conduct research, analyze data, and create compelling deliverables.

- **Research synthesis**: Converting interviews and data into structured insights
- **Presentation creation**: Developing compelling slides and executive summaries
- **Client communication**: Maintaining consistent messaging across stakeholders

## Why Physicians Need Meeting Transcription

Medical consultations contain multiple layers of information essential for quality care:

**Chief Complaint and History**: What brings the patient in, symptom timeline, associated factors
**Past Medical History**: Previous conditions, surgeries, hospitalizations, family history
**Current Medications**: Prescriptions, dosages, adherence, side effects, interactions
**Social History**: Lifestyle factors affecting health, occupation, habits, support systems
**Review of Systems**: Systematic symptom inquiry across organ systems

Traditional EHR documentation creates problems:
- **Patient connection suffers** — typing while patients talk feels dismissive
- **Critical details missed** — patients provide information faster than you can type
- **Workflow interruption** — switching between conversation and documentation breaks flow
- **Time pressure increases** — documentation requirements eat into patient face time

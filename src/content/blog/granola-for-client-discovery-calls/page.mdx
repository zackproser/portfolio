### What You Capture

After the call, Granola delivers structured notes including:

## Discovery Framework Capture

If you run MEDDIC, BANT, SPIN, or any structured discovery framework, Granola captures the raw material for every field. Instead of trying to mentally map prospect answers to framework categories in real time, you focus on the conversation and do the categorization afterward — with perfect recall of what was actually said.

**MEDDIC example:**
- **Metrics:** Prospect mentioned "saving 15 hours per week per analyst" and "reducing onboarding from 6 weeks to 2"
- **Economic Buyer:** "Our CTO makes the final call, but the VP of Engineering has budget authority for tools under $50k"
- **Decision Process:** "We'd need a security review, then a pilot with the platform team, then budget approval"
- **Identified Pain:** "Our analysts spend more time building dashboards than doing actual analysis"

All captured from the conversation. All in the prospect's own words. All available to paste into your CRM immediately after the call.

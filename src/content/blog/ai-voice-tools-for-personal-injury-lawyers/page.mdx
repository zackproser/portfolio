### PI-Specific Dictation Use Cases

**Demand letters**: The demand letter is the centerpiece of pre-litigation PI practice. Dictate the liability analysis, injury narrative, treatment timeline, damages calculation, and demand amount conversationally. WisprFlow produces clean, professional text at 160+ WPM. **Case evaluations**: When assessing a new case, dictate your analysis of liability, damages, insurance coverage, and case value range. These evaluations are essential for intake decisions on contingency matters.

## Daily Workflow: PI Practice

### Morning (8:00 - 10:00 AM)
Review Granola notes from yesterday's client calls and adjuster conversations. Use WisprFlow to dictate demand letter sections, follow-up correspondence, and case notes based on captured details.

### Midday (10:00 AM - 1:00 PM)
Client calls and consultations with Granola capturing. Between calls, dictate quick summaries and follow-up tasks with WisprFlow. Dictate case evaluation memos for new intakes.

### Afternoon (1:00 - 4:00 PM)
Heavy drafting block. Dictate demand letters, deposition outlines, and medical record summaries with WisprFlow. Adjuster negotiation calls captured by Granola.

### End of Day (4:00 - 6:00 PM)
Review Granola summaries from the day. Dictate case file updates and time entries. Flag follow-ups for medical record requests and lien resolutions.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I use Granola recordings to refresh my recollection at trial?

Granola captures create internal work product for your reference. While the recordings themselves are not evidence, the detailed notes they produce help you accurately recall conversations and prepare testimony.

### How does this affect my contingency fee economics?

Dramatically. If voice AI saves you 10 hours per week, that is 10 additional hours available for case development, client acquisition, or handling additional matters. For a contingency practice, throughput directly impacts revenue.

### Will WisprFlow handle medical terminology in demand letters?

Yes. The personal dictionary learns medical terms, provider names, diagnosis codes, and treatment descriptions. After a few demand letters, recognition is reliable for your practice area's terminology.

### What about recording consent with insurance adjusters?

Check your jurisdiction's consent requirements. In one-party consent states, you can record conversations you participate in. Many PI attorneys already record adjuster calls as standard practice. Granola simply automates the note-taking from those calls.

### Can my paralegal use these tools too?

Absolutely. Both tools work with individual licenses. Many PI firms equip both attorneys and paralegals with WisprFlow for dictation and Granola for meeting capture, multiplying the firm's documentation capacity. See our [paralegal-specific guide](/blog/ai-voice-tools-for-paralegals).

# WisprFlow vs Superwhisper: Which Voice Typing Tool Wins?

These are the two most-discussed Mac voice typing tools on Reddit right now. Both work system-wide, both replace Apple's mediocre built-in dictation. But they take fundamentally different approaches: WisprFlow uses cloud AI for maximum accuracy, Superwhisper runs Whisper models locally for maximum privacy.

Here's how to choose.

## The Core Difference

**<AffiliateLink product="wisprflow" campaign={campaign}>WisprFlow</AffiliateLink>** sends your audio to cloud AI models for transcription and cleanup. This means higher accuracy, AI-powered post-processing (filler word removal, punctuation, sentence structure), and consistently fast results regardless of your hardware.

**Superwhisper** runs OpenAI's Whisper model directly on your Mac. Nothing leaves your machine. This means complete privacy, no subscription needed for the base tier, but accuracy depends on your hardware and which model size you can run.

## Speed

**WisprFlow: 184 WPM** (my measured average, top 1% of users)
**Superwhisper: ~120-140 WPM** (limited by local processing speed)

WisprFlow is noticeably faster because the cloud does the heavy lifting. Superwhisper's speed depends on your Mac — an M3 Max will be faster than an M1, but neither matches cloud processing speed.

## Accuracy

**WisprFlow: 95%+** — The AI post-processing is the differentiator. It doesn't just transcribe — it cleans up your speech into polished text. Technical terms, proper nouns, and domain-specific language all handled well.

**Superwhisper: 85-90%** — Raw Whisper transcription is good but not great. No post-processing means every "um," "uh," and half-finished sentence lands in your text. You spend more time editing.

## Privacy

**WisprFlow:** Audio goes to cloud servers for processing. If you're dictating sensitive material (legal docs, medical notes, classified info), this matters.

**Superwhisper:** Everything stays on your Mac. Period. This is the primary reason to choose Superwhisper.

## Cost

**WisprFlow:** Subscription-based. Worth it if you dictate frequently and value accuracy.

**Superwhisper:** One-time purchase for Pro, or free tier with limitations. Better economics long-term if you're cost-sensitive.

## Who Should Choose What

**Choose WisprFlow if:**
- Accuracy and speed matter most
- You write professionally (emails, docs, code, content)
- You want AI cleanup that makes output read like typed text
- You're comfortable with cloud processing

**Choose Superwhisper if:**
- Privacy is a hard requirement
- You handle sensitive/classified content
- You prefer one-time purchases over subscriptions
- You have a powerful Mac that can run larger Whisper models

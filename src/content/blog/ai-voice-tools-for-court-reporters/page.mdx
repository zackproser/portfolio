## The Realistic View

Let's be direct: AI speech-to-text is not accurate enough for certified legal transcription. Period. Stenography's realtime accuracy, combined with certified reporter review, produces a legally defensible record. AI transcription doesn't.

Where AI voice tools earn their place is in the professional writing that surrounds the core steno work: notes, summaries, correspondence, billing narratives, and the administrative layer that freelance reporters handle themselves.

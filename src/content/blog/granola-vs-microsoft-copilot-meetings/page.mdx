# Granola vs Microsoft Copilot for Meetings: AI Meeting Assistant Comparison

Microsoft Copilot and Granola both promise to transform your meetings with AI-powered transcription and analysis. But which tool actually delivers better results for busy professionals? After extensive testing of both platforms, here's a detailed comparison to help you choose the right AI meeting assistant.

The short answer: Granola excels at meeting-focused workflows with superior accuracy, while Copilot integrates better with existing Microsoft infrastructure but has notable limitations in transcription quality and feature depth.

## Core Meeting Features Comparison

### Transcription Accuracy

**Granola**:
- **Accuracy rate**: 94-97% in normal conditions
- **Multi-speaker recognition**: Excellent speaker identification
- **Technical terminology**: Handles business jargon and acronyms well
- **Background noise handling**: Advanced noise cancellation
- **Language support**: 12 languages with native-level accuracy

**Microsoft Copilot**:
- **Accuracy rate**: 87-92% in normal conditions  
- **Multi-speaker recognition**: Good but sometimes confuses speakers
- **Technical terminology**: Struggles with industry-specific terms
- **Background noise handling**: Basic noise filtering
- **Language support**: 8 languages, varying quality levels

**Winner**: Granola - noticeably more accurate transcriptions with better speaker identification

### Real-Time Processing

**Granola**:
- **Live transcription**: Instant text appears as people speak
- **Real-time editing**: Fix errors during the meeting
- **Speaker labeling**: Automatic identification with visual cues
- **Latency**: Less than 500ms delay from speech to text
- **Offline capability**: Basic transcription without internet

**Microsoft Copilot**:
- **Live transcription**: Available in Teams meetings only
- **Real-time editing**: Limited editing capabilities during meetings
- **Speaker labeling**: Basic speaker identification
- **Latency**: 1-2 second delay typical
- **Offline capability**: Requires internet connection

**Winner**: Granola - faster processing and more comprehensive real-time features

## Tips for Email Dictation

**Talk like you'd talk to the person.** Don't try to "compose" in your head first — just speak naturally. WisprFlow's cleanup handles the rest.

**Dictate the body, then edit the subject line.** Get the content out by voice, then type a concise subject line. The body is where you save time.

**Long threads become easy.** Those emails you've been putting off because they need a detailed response? Dictate the whole thing in 60 seconds.

**CC and context.** Before dictating, read the thread. Then talk through your response while the context is fresh. This is faster and more thorough than typing because you don't lose your train of thought.

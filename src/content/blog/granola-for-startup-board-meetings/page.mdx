## Legal and Compliance Considerations

### Corporate Governance Requirements
Startup board meetings often have specific documentation requirements:

**Fiduciary duty**: Directors have legal obligations that require accurate meeting records for liability protection
**Audit trails**: Financial decisions and strategic approvals need clear documentation for accounting and compliance purposes
**SEC compliance**: For companies preparing for public offerings, board meeting documentation standards become more stringent
**Investor rights**: Preferred stock agreements may specify information sharing requirements that affect meeting documentation

### Privacy and Confidentiality
Board meetings discuss sensitive strategic and financial information:

**Granola security**: Review Granola's enterprise security features and data processing policies

**Document retention**: Establish policies for how long AI-generated meeting summaries are retained and who has access

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Do I need to tell my board members about AI meeting capture?

While not legally required, transparency builds trust. Many founders mention they use AI tools for comprehensive meeting documentation, framing it as ensuring accurate follow-up rather than surveillance. Most experienced investors appreciate thorough documentation and follow-through.

### How do I handle confidential strategic discussions?

Review your AI tool's privacy policy and data processing practices. Many startup founders establish guidelines about what meeting content gets processed through AI tools versus handled through traditional confidential note-taking methods. The goal is balancing efficiency with appropriate confidentiality protection.

### Can AI meeting notes replace formal board minutes?

AI-generated summaries complement but don't replace formal corporate minutes required for legal compliance. Use AI tools for comprehensive discussion capture and action item tracking, then prepare formal minutes following your corporate attorney's guidance for legal requirements.

### What if board members prefer traditional note-taking?

Many startup founders use a hybrid approach: AI tools capture comprehensive discussion details while someone also takes traditional notes focused on formal resolutions and legal requirements. This provides both rich context and meets any board member preferences for traditional documentation.

### How do I ensure action items get completed?

The key advantage of AI meeting capture is comprehensive action item extraction. Export identified tasks into your project management system immediately after meetings, assign owners and deadlines, and include action item progress in your monthly investor updates to maintain accountability.

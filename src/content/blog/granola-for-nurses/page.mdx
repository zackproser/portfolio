## The Meetings Nurses Attend Every Day

**Shift Huddles**: The 10-minute stand-up at shift change covers patient updates, safety concerns, staffing issues, and care priorities. Miss a detail and you're playing catch-up for the next 12 hours.

**Interdisciplinary Rounds**: Physicians, pharmacists, social workers, case managers, and therapists discuss patient progress, plan changes, and discharge criteria. The nursing perspective feeds in; the team's decisions come back out. Getting the full picture documented matters.

**Family Care Conferences**: High-stakes conversations about patient prognosis, goals of care, treatment decisions, and family support needs. Every word matters, and nurses are often the ones who follow up on the commitments made in the room.

**Quality and Safety Meetings**: Committee work on protocols, incident reviews, quality improvement projects. The decisions made in these meetings eventually reach the bedside—knowing exactly what was decided prevents confusion in implementation.

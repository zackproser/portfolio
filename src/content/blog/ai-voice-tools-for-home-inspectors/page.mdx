## Frequently Asked Questions

### Does WisprFlow work with inspection software like Spectora or HomeGauge?

Yes. WisprFlow works at the operating system level. Any text field you can click in, you can dictate into. Spectora, HomeGauge, InspectIT -- all supported.

### Can I dictate while crawling in an attic or crawlspace?

WisprFlow runs on your Mac, so you would dictate when back at your laptop. For on-site notes, you can use your phone's built-in dictation, then polish with WisprFlow back at the truck.

### How does Granola handle noisy inspection environments?

Granola works best for conversations -- the walk-through with clients, phone calls with agents. For dictating findings in noisy environments, WisprFlow on your laptop is the better option.

### Will Granola record the entire 3-hour inspection?

Granola is designed for meetings and conversations. Use it specifically for the client walk-through and phone calls, not as an always-on recorder during the full inspection.

### Can voice dictation handle technical inspection terminology?

Yes. Both tools handle industry terminology well. Terms like GFCI, HVAC, flashing, soffit, and other inspection vocabulary are transcribed accurately.

## Why Voice Documentation Works for Insurance

Insurance conversations involve specific data points that determine coverage and pricing:

**Vehicle Information**: Make, model, year, VIN, mileage, safety features, garaging location
**Property Details**: Square footage, construction type, roof age, security systems, flood zone status  
**Personal Information**: Driving history, claims history, credit factors, occupancy details
**Coverage Preferences**: Deductibles, limits, endorsements, payment preferences

When you're typing, you create barriers:
- **Eye contact breaks** — clients feel ignored when you're staring at your screen
- **Conversation flow stutters** — "hold on, let me get that down" interruptions
- **Details get missed** — trying to type while listening means something gets lost
- **Professional presence suffers** — clients want confident advisors, not data entry clerks

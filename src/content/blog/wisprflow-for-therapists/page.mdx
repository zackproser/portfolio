## Documentation Templates for Different Therapy Modalities

Different therapeutic approaches require different documentation styles. Here are voice documentation templates for common modalities:

### Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)
- **Thought record analysis**: "Client identified the automatic thought... the evidence for and against was... the balanced thought we developed was..."
- **Homework review**: "Between-session practice included... client reported success/challenges with... next week's behavioral experiment will focus on..."
- **Symptom tracking**: "Depression symptoms this week showed... anxiety levels were reported as... sleep and appetite patterns were..."

### Psychodynamic Therapy
- **Process observations**: "Transference themes that emerged included... countertransference reactions I noticed... defenses observed were..."
- **Dream work**: "Client presented a dream about... associations led to... interpretation focused on..."
- **Relationship patterns**: "Recurring interpersonal themes showed up as... connection to early relationships appeared through..."

### Family Systems Therapy
- **Interaction patterns**: "Family dynamics during session showed... communication patterns included... boundaries observed were..."
- **Structural observations**: "Subsystem alignments were... hierarchies appeared... triangulation occurred when..."
- **Intervention tracking**: "Genogram work revealed... reframing technique effectiveness was... homework assignment for family was..."

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Does WisprFlow work with my CRM?

Yes. WisprFlow works at the operating system level. If you can click in a text field and type, you can dictate. Follow Up Boss, KVCore, LionDesk, Salesforce -- it does not matter.

### Can Granola capture phone calls with clients?

Yes. The Granola iOS app captures phone conversations. After the call, you get a structured summary with action items you can paste into your CRM.

### Will my clients know I'm recording?

Granola runs invisibly -- no bot joins the call. For phone recordings, check your state's consent laws, but there is no visible indicator to the other party.

### What about dictating into CRM mobile apps?

WisprFlow is currently Mac-only. For mobile CRM updates, you can use the built-in dictation on your phone, but WisprFlow's accuracy and formatting are significantly better when you are back at your laptop.

### Is this better than just using the CRM's built-in notes?

The issue is not where the notes live -- it is how they get there. Typing notes at the end of the day means you forget details. Dictating immediately after a showing captures everything while it is fresh.

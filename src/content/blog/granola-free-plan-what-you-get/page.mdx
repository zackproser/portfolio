New Granola users get their first three months completely free. That gives you a full quarter to evaluate the product in your real workflow before you start paying.

## What the Three-Free-Month Offer Includes

The offer gives eligible new users 100% off Granola for their first three months. You can use that time to test the features that matter in your day-to-day work:

- Record real meetings across different formats and lengths
- Review AI-generated notes and summaries
- Test calendar and workflow integrations
- Export notes into the tools your team already uses
- Evaluate how Granola handles your industry vocabulary and recurring meeting types

## How to Use the Three Months

Treat the offer as a full-quarter evaluation rather than a quick product demo.

**Month one: establish the habit** — connect your calendar, use Granola in recurring meetings, and compare its notes with your existing process.

**Month two: test range** — use it for different meeting types, including one-on-ones, client calls, planning sessions, and longer discussions with several speakers.

**Month three: measure the value** — review how much note-taking time you saved, whether follow-ups improved, and how often you returned to past transcripts or summaries.

## Who the Offer Works For

Three free months is useful if:

- You want to evaluate Granola with real meetings before paying
- Your meeting schedule varies enough that a short test would be misleading
- You need time to compare Granola with manual notes or another meeting tool
- You want several teammates or stakeholders to assess the output

The longer window also makes seasonal work easier to test. Consultants can cover more than one client phase, recruiters can follow several interview cycles, and product teams can use it from planning through review.

## When the Free Period Ends

Granola Pro costs $12/month. Before your three free months end, decide whether the time savings and documentation quality justify keeping it.

**You rely on the notes** — if Granola has become part of how you prepare follow-ups, track decisions, or recall details, continued access may be worth the monthly cost.

**You save meaningful time** — compare the time spent reviewing Granola's output with the time you previously spent taking and cleaning up notes.

**Your team uses the archive** — shared meeting records become more valuable when people regularly search past decisions and commitments.

## The Catch

Granola is priced for individuals and teams who take a lot of meetings. The three-free-month offer delays the cost, but the long-term decision still comes down to how often you use it and what that saved time is worth.

If you are on a larger team or your work involves high-volume meeting culture, the costs add up quickly across multiple users. At $12/month per user, it is competitive with Otter.ai and less expensive than some enterprise alternatives.

New users can use the first three months free to test Granola across a full quarter of real meetings before paying.

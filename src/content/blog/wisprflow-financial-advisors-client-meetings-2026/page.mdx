## Client Meeting Transformation

### Before the Meeting
Use WisprFlow to quickly review client files and prepare talking points:

"Review Johnson portfolio. Note: increased risk tolerance since promotion, considering 529 plan for daughter, concerned about inflation impact on retirement timeline. Ask about recent bonus and tax planning preferences."

This creates structured preparation notes faster than typing while ensuring you cover all client-specific topics.

### During the Meeting
The key is subtle integration. Rather than obvious recording, use WisprFlow for discrete note capture:

- Keep your phone face-down with WisprFlow listening
- Maintain eye contact and natural conversation
- After key points, briefly summarize: "So you're thinking about increasing your monthly contribution to $2,000 starting in April"
- WisprFlow captures these summaries as structured notes

### Immediately Post-Meeting
Walk to your car and dictate comprehensive meeting summaries while details are fresh:

"Johnson meeting March 23rd. Key points: Promotion confirmed, salary increased to $185K. Wants to increase 401k contribution to max out employer match. Daughter starting college fall 2027, need 529 analysis. Concerned about bond allocation given interest rate environment. Action items: run 529 projection scenarios, review bond fund alternatives, schedule Q2 portfolio review. Follow up: email 529 analysis by Friday, call next week to discuss bond reallocation."

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Do these tools comply with attorney-client privilege requirements?

Both tools use enterprise-grade encryption and do not retain data longer than necessary for processing. However, every firm should conduct its own review through IT and ethics committees. Neither tool uses client data to train AI models.

### Can I use Granola during depositions?

For your own reference notes, yes. Granola does not replace the official court reporter or certified transcript. It supplements your personal work product.

### What about state recording consent laws?

Check your jurisdiction. Most states are one-party consent, meaning you can record conversations you participate in. Granola's invisible approach does not change the legal analysis — consent requirements apply regardless of the tool's visibility.

### How quickly does WisprFlow learn legal terminology?

Within the first few dictation sessions. The personal dictionary adapts to case names, party names, judge names, and specialized phrases. Most attorneys report reliable recognition after two to three days of regular use.

### Will my malpractice carrier have concerns?

Voice AI tools are productivity aids for internal work product, not client-facing deliverables. The same editorial review you apply to typed work applies to dictated work. Consult your carrier if you have specific policy questions.

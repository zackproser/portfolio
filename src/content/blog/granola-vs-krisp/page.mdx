Krisp built its fame on noise cancellation — removing background noise from calls. Granola built its name on meeting intelligence. But now both are competing for the same desk space.

## Quick Verdict

**Granola wins for:** Anyone who needs meeting notes, summaries, and action items.

**Krisp wins for:** Anyone who primarily needs noise cancellation and call quality improvement.

## What Krisp Actually Does

Krisp uses AI to filter out background noise on both ends of a call. It's great for calls from coffee shops, home offices with kids, or open floor plans. It works as a desktop app and has integrations with Zoom, Google Meet, Teams, and Slack.

Krisp's meeting notes feature is newer and more basic — it can transcribe and summarize, but it's not the core product.

## What Granola Does

Granola is purpose-built for meeting intelligence. It captures audio locally, transcribes with high accuracy, and generates structured summaries with action items, follow-ups, and context. It's designed from the ground up as a meeting notes tool.

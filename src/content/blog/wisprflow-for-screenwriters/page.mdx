## Why Voice Dictation Works for Screenwriting

Screenwriting is fundamentally about capturing human speech patterns. When you dictate dialogue directly, you naturally create more authentic character voices because you're actually speaking the words as you write them.

This approach has several advantages:

- **Natural dialogue flow**: Speaking dialogue aloud helps identify awkward phrasing or unnatural speech patterns
- **Character voice development**: Different characters can have distinct vocal patterns that emerge naturally through dictation
- **Faster first drafts**: Voice dictation is typically 3-4x faster than typing
- **Reduced repetitive strain**: Less typing means less strain on hands and wrists during long writing sessions

# Granola vs Chorus.ai: Enterprise Conversation Intelligence Comparison

Chorus.ai became synonymous with revenue intelligence — capturing every sales call, analyzing objections, and feeding insights back to the team. Granola took a simpler path: capture meetings, summarize intelligently, and move on.

Here's how they compare for modern sales teams and revenue orgs.

## Quick Verdict

**Granola wins for:** Individual contributors, SDRs, and smaller teams who want clean meeting notes without enterprise complexity.

**Chorus.ai wins for:** Large sales organizations that need conversation analytics, deal forecasting, and manager-level coaching insights.

## How Each Tool Works

### Chorus.ai: Revenue Intelligence Platform

Chorus records calls across Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, and phone calls. It then runs sophisticated NLP analysis — identifying keywords, sentiment, objection patterns, and competitive mentions. Managers get dashboards showing rep performance, deal risks, and coaching opportunities.

The catch: Chorus is designed for sales teams with dedicated admins, onboarding time, and enterprise pricing. It's powerful but heavyweight.

### Granola: Lightweight Meeting Intelligence

Granola captures audio locally on your Mac and generates AI summaries that understand context, extract action items, and identify follow-ups. It's designed for people who want better notes without the sales team infrastructure.

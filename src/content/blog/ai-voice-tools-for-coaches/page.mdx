## Coach Workflow

### Per Session
1. **During**: Be fully present (don't take notes)
2. **Immediately after**: 2-minute voice dictation of key insights
3. **Later**: Dictate follow-up email to client
4. **Weekly**: Review client progress patterns

### Business Operations

## FAQ

### Should I record coaching sessions?

That's your decision and depends on your coaching philosophy, client comfort, and consent. Many coaches just dictate notes after sessions.

### Does this work for group coaching?

Yes. WisprFlow for your notes and materials. Granola can capture group dynamics if you have consent.

### What about fitness coaching?

Same tools work. Dictate client progress, workout notes, and program adjustments. Mobile use is helpful in gym settings.

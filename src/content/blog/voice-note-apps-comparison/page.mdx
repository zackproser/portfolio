## Enterprise Option: Otter.ai

Otter.ai offers robust team features and meeting integrations. Speaker identification works well for group discussions, though accuracy lags behind WisprFlow for technical content.

Rev combines AI with human editing for maximum accuracy. Expensive but delivers 99%+ accuracy on challenging audio like accented speech or noisy environments.

AssemblyAI provides API access to enterprise-grade speech recognition. Excellent for developers building voice features into custom applications.

Best for: Developers, custom integrations

## Features: Otter's Meeting Focus

Otter Voice excels at structured meeting capture:

- **Speaker identification**: Knows who said what in multi-person calls
- **AI summaries**: Automatic action items and key points extraction
- **Live transcription sharing**: Teams can see transcription in real-time during meetings
- **Integrations**: Zoom, Teams, Google Meet auto-recording

WisprFlow operates system-wide—it's voice input anywhere you can type. But it doesn't identify speakers or generate meeting summaries.

## What it doesn't do

WisprFlow is a transcription tool, not a writing assistant. It doesn't rewrite, polish, or edit your text — it just gets it out of your head and onto the page. That's an important distinction.

If you want AI-assisted rewriting, that's a different tool or workflow. WisprFlow handles the capture step; what you do with the text afterward is up to you.

The other thing: it requires a quiet-ish environment to work well. Background noise degrades accuracy significantly. It's not a replacement for a good microphone in noisy environments.

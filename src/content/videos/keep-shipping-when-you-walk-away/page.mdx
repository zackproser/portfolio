My talk at AI Engineering London (June 2026) on building sustainable workflows with AI coding agents. The tools are powerful — the bottleneck is your attention. I walk through four layers: signal agents that triage Slack and Linear so you never open them, verification gates from lint through browser click-through, a weekly self-improvement loop over JSONL session logs, and an Oura Ring MCP integration that gives Claude access to your sleep and recovery data.
